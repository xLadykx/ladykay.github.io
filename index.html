Meet Kaya

Hey, I’m Kaya — a photographer with a big heart, a curious eye, and a love for capturing life’s most meaningful moments. While I’m still early in my journey, I bring passion, care, and a relaxed energy to every session.

I work with families, couples, and individuals on everything from maternity shoots and birthday celebrations to timeless portraits and real, everyday magic. I focus on the feeling — the laughter, the stillness, the in-betweens — because that’s where the real story lives.

My approach is warm, easygoing, and centered around making you feel seen, comfortable, and confident. No stiff poses, no pressure — just authentic connection and photos that reflect the heart of who you are.

Every session is personal to me. I want you to walk away with images that feel honest, joyful, and uniquely yours. If that sounds like your vibe, let’s create something beautiful together.
